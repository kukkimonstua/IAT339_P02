Colour Scheme

We at Stylish Sheets want for our products to be colourful, but not overpowering. Therefore our selection includes a wide varity of pale, pastel, and muted tones. We want our products to be professionally colourful rather than childlike colourful, to fit with nay kind of workstyle.

The pinks will be used for links

The greens will be used for buttons

The blues will be used for additional accents to add variety

The pale yellows will be the background elements

The web-pages will use either white or black text, whichever is more viewable
